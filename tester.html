The Four Domains

The logic gates of human execution.

Domain 01
System: The state of the world you need to see before you can even begin to process a task.

Order [B]

Operates through quantified variables, clear hierarchies, and established rules.

Chaos [A]

Anti-fragile. Thrives in the noise. Prefers organic, shifting environments where they create their own gravity.

Domain 02
Environment: The specific pressure-setting required for your engine to activate.

Restriction [B]

The Clutch Performer. Requires lethal stakes and hard deadlines to activate.

Freedom [A]

The Marathon Performer. Needs space to breathe and infinite options to explore. Pressure stalls them.

Domain 03
Acquisition: The method by which you gain mastery over a system and execute solutions.

Learner [B]

The Sponge. Masters systems through methodical study, frameworks, and "paying dues." Power is cumulative.

Genius [A]

The Oracle. Talent is innate and intuitive. Skips steps and solves via "Blackout" flow states.

Domain 04
Motivation: Why you are playing the game and what kind of reward sustains your long-term effort and prevents burnout.

World-Type [B]

Mission-Driven. Seeks legitimacy and a defined role. Fulfillment comes from serving a cause or legacy.

Self-Type [A]

Ego-Driven. Seeks personal mastery, pride, and the thrill of the "Self." Plays to prove personal excellence.
